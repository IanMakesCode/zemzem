About Divine eSports & Coaching

Founded in 2022 by a group of Grand Champion players, Divine eSports & Coaching was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to high-quality Rocket League training, regardless of their financial situation.

Our founders, who collectively have over 15,000 hours in Rocket League and have competed in RLCS qualifiers, noticed a gap in the community. While expensive coaching services existed, there was no comprehensive, free platform that could help players of all skill levels improve systematically.

What started as a small Discord server with 50 members has grown into a thriving community of over 2,000 active players and multiple competitive esports teams. Our unique tier-based progression system ensures that every member receives coaching appropriate to their skill level and commitment to improvement.

Today, Divine eSports & Coaching is proud to have helped over 5,000 players rank up, with many achieving their dream ranks and some even going on to compete professionally on our esports rosters.

Our Coaching Methods

🎯

Personalized Analysis

Every player receives a custom analysis of their gameplay, identifying specific areas for improvement based on their current rank and playstyle.

📹

Replay Reviews

Our coaches analyze your replays in detail, providing timestamped feedback on positioning, decision-making, and mechanical execution.

🏋️

Custom Training Packs

Receive training packs specifically designed for your skill level and the mechanics you need to master for your next rank up.

Live Coaching Sessions

One-on-one sessions where coaches watch you play in real-time, providing instant feedback and guidance during matches.

📚

Educational Resources

Access our extensive library of guides, tutorials, and educational content covering everything from basic mechanics to advanced strategies.

👥

Group Training
