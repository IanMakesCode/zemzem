About Me

I'm Zemi, a high-ranked Rocket League player and coach focused on helping players reach their full potential. My coaching approach combines statistical analysis with personalized training routines to address your specific needs and improve your gameplay.

With peak ranks of Supersonic Legend in 1v1 and 2v2, and Grand Champion 3 in 3v3, I have the experience and knowledge to help you improve in any game mode. I understand the mechanics, positioning, and decision-making required at the highest levels of play.

Whether you're looking to improve your mechanical skills, game sense, or overall strategy, I provide tailored coaching that will help you climb the ranks and become a more confident player.
